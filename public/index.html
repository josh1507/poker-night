Poker Night

Multiplayer Texas Hold'em

How to Play

  1. 1 One person taps Host a Game to create a room and get a 4-letter code.
  2. 2 Everyone else taps Join a Table and enters the code on their phone.
  3. 3 Open Table View on a TV or large screen so everyone can see the community cards.
  4. 4 The host deals when everyone's in. Your hole cards show on your phone — keep them secret!
